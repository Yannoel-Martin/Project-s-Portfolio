## Project details
Separated into two blocks:
1. public : containing what is "front", i.e.:
    1. pages
    1. templates
    1. resources (images and other files to load)
1. system: containing what is "back", i.e.:
    1. functions
    1. constants
    1. languages ​​(for translations)
